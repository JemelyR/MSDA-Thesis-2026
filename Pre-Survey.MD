# Pre-Study Survey

You are invited to take part in a research study being done by **Jemely Robles** (PI **Nikhil Singh**) at Dartmouth College. The purpose of this study is to better understand how physical sensation affects virtual reality experiences.

If you choose to participate, you will first fill out a ~5-minute questionnaire, then watch a short virtual reality experience, and you may feel gentle physical sensations on your hands and face. These sensations are part of the study and are not harmful, but you will not be able to visibly see them coming. The experience will take ~3 minutes to complete, followed by a ~5-minute post-experience survey.

Some people may experience slight motion sickness or dizziness while using a VR headset. This is a normal and temporary effect that usually goes away after removing the headset. You may stop participating at any time if you feel uncomfortable.

There are no known risks beyond those you might face in everyday life. You may stop participating at any time without penalty. Your responses will be anonymous and will not be linked to your identity. You will only be asked to provide your given participant ID to track your responses.

By selecting "Next" you indicate that you have read this information and agree to take part in the study.

> **Questions?** Please contact Nikhil Singh at nikhil.u.singh@dartmouth.edu or Jemely Robles at Jemely.Robles.gr@dartmouth.edu. If you have questions, concerns, complaints, or suggestions about human research at Dartmouth, you may call the Office of the Committee for the Protection of Human Subjects at Dartmouth College (603) 646-6482 during normal business hours.
